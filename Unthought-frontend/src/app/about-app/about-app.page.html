Unthought
 Unthought is an App that is focused on breaking the above-mentioned myths and problems for aspiring entrepreneurs by making a platform that helps users at every step of their entrepreneurship journey - from getting the idea all the way till finding invertor and everything in between.
“Unthought” can be used by anyone and everyone who are interested in entrepreneurship or who have already started their entrepreneur journey. This App can also be used by Students and young Graduates to gain some experience and skill by joining hands with many of the on going projects. There will also be a separate portal for mentors. Various mentors from different sectors can use this app and guide students in their projects or can start and work on their own idea or project. Finally, there will be a third portal for investors. Where they will be shown the projects which are at final stages and are looking for investment.
